Sumo, food, and Japanese culture

A rare Osaka dining experience for international visitors

Osaka Sumo Restaurant Ochiizumibeya brings Japanese cuisine and the tradition of sumo wrestling together. A sumo ring sits in the center of the restaurant, so guests can watch powerful live performances up close while they dine.

The show is performed by former professional sumo wrestlers and includes matches, cultural explanations, and interactive moments with guests. Plans include show-only tickets, Chanko-nabe dining, selected Halal Wagyu Sukiyaki, and premium courses that may include sushi.
